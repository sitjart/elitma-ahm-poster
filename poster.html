By the Nodes, for the Nodes. Building ELIXIR management capacity.

As the ELIXIR network grows, effective coordination and management are essential. ELITMa equips Node staff with practical skills and shared approaches to support planning, delivery and collaboration across the infrastructure.

Explore ELITMa's modules

Ten modules – built by the ELIXIR community for the ELIXIR community – covering the management skills Nodes need to plan, run and grow their work.

Node data management strategy

delivered in 2023 delivered in 2025 next: 30 Sept 2026

Supports the development of robust data management strategies within ELIXIR Nodes, focusing on best practices from ELIXIR's data management tools and services. Next delivery on 30 September 2026, co-located with the RDM Community event.

Module leads:
  • Mijke Jetten (ELIXIR‑NL)
  • Celia van Gelder (ELIXIR‑NL)
  • RDM Community

Hub contact: Fabio Liberante

Communication

blueprint ready ready for Node success stories

Effective internal communication. An asynchronous module to be owned by the comms group after the v1 release, catering for new comms officers joining or anyone in ELIXIR needing support in this area. Aligns with the STEERS comms toolkit and prior documents on communications best practices in ELIXIR.

In the People Tier 2027–2028

Consolidation actions for ELITMa are currently proposed for the 27–28 Programme.

In summary

ELITMa addresses two of the ambitions of the People Tier in the ELIXIR Scientific Programme 2024–28: that Nodes are well equipped to efficiently manage and run a distributed infrastructure; and that ELIXIR technical and scientific staff have the skills needed for operational excellence and impact.
