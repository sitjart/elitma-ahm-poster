All Hands · 2026

By the Nodes, for the Nodes. Building ELIXIR management capacity.

As the ELIXIR network grows, effective coordination and management are essential. ELITMa equips Node staff with practical skills and shared approaches to support planning, delivery and collaboration across the infrastructure.

Explore ELITMa's modules

Ten modules — built by the ELIXIR community for the ELIXIR community — covering the management skills Nodes need to plan, run and grow their work.

ELIXIR introduction & governance

A comprehensive introduction to European and global research infrastructures, ELIXIR's work, and practical tools and best practices.
